¿ What is 'Una historia bélica' ?

"Una historia bélica" is a 2D platformer video game that follows the thrilling adventure of a brave father on his quest to rescue his kidnapped daughter. In the game, players must navigate through challenging levels, collect coins, and overcome obstacles to progress in the story. Each level is filled with dangers and enemies, and the main goal is to gather enough coins to unlock the next stage and ultimately save the protagonist’s daughter. With a blend of action, platforming, and collection, "Una historia bélica" offers a dynamic and immersive experience.

Features

  • Challenging Levels: Explore a variety of levels with progressive difficulty, each designed to offer new challenges and keep the gameplay exciting.
  • Coin Collection: Collect coins throughout the levels to unlock new stages and enhance the protagonist’s abilities.
  • Obstacles and Enemies: Face a variety of obstacles and enemies that will test your platforming skills and strategy.
  • Engaging Storyline: Experience an immersive narrative that follows a father's heroic quest to rescue his kidnapped daughter.
  • Upgrades and Powers: Unlock upgrades and special abilities by collecting coins to assist in your mission and overcome challenges.
  • Attractive 2D Graphics: Enjoy vibrant and detailed visual design that complements the gaming experience.
  • Music and Sound Effects: Immerse yourself in the action with an exciting soundtrack and immersive sound effects that enhance the gameplay experience.

Installation Requirements

  • For Windows: The game requires Windows 10 or later, an Intel Core i3 processor or equivalent, 4 GB of RAM a DirectX 11 compatible graphics card, and 500 MB of available storage space. A keyboard and mouse are also needed.
  • For Android: game requires Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later, a quad-core 1.5 GHz processor or faster, 2 GB of RAM, an Adreno 506 graphics chip or equivalent, and 500 MB of available storage space. The game is designed for touchscreen input.
